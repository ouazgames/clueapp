## Sample Hint Patterns for Translation

### Pattern 1: "Exactly N [type] in [location] is neighboring [NAME]"
- EN: "Exactly 1 innocent in column A is neighboring Megan"
- AR: "بالضبط 1 بريء في العمود A جار لـ Megan"
- ES: "Exactamente 1 inocente en la columna A es vecino de Megan"

### Pattern 2: "[NAME] is one of N [type] on the edges"
- EN: "Zoe is one of 6 innocents on the edges"
- AR: "Zoe واحد من 6 أبرياء على الحواف"
- ES: "Zoe es uno de 6 inocentes en los bordes"

### Pattern 3: "[NAME] has more [type] neighbors than [NAME]"
- EN: "Xavi has more innocent neighbors than Isaac"
- AR: "Xavi لديه جيران أبرياء أكثر من Isaac"
- ES: "Xavi tiene más vecinos inocentes que Isaac"

### Pattern 4: "There are exactly N [type] between [NAME] and [NAME]"
- EN: "There are exactly 2 innocents between Nancy and Pam"
- AR: "يوجد بالضبط 2 أبرياء بين Nancy و Pam"
- ES: "Hay exactamente 2 inocentes entre Nancy y Pam"

### Pattern 5: "Column/Row [X] has more [type] than any other column/row"
- EN: "Column D has more criminals than any other column"
- AR: "العمود D يحتوي على مجرمين أكثر من أي عمود آخر"
- ES: "La columna D tiene más criminales que cualquier otra columna"

### Pattern 6: "[NAME] and [NAME] have an equal number of [type] neighbors"
- EN: "Wanda and Gary have an equal number of innocent neighbors"
- AR: "Wanda و Gary لديهما عدد متساوٍ من الجيران الأبرياء"
- ES: "Wanda y Gary tienen igual número de vecinos inocentes"

### Pattern 7: "Both [type] in [location] are [NAME]'s neighbors"
- EN: "Both criminals in row 4 are Xavi's neighbors"
- AR: "كلا المجرمين في الصف 4 هما جيران Xavi"
- ES: "Ambos criminales en la fila 4 son vecinos de Xavi"

### Pattern 8: "All [type] between [NAME] and [NAME] are connected"
- EN: "All criminals between Nancy and Ryan are connected"
- AR: "كل المجرمين بين Nancy و Ryan متصلون"
- ES: "Todos los criminales entre Nancy y Ryan están conectados"
